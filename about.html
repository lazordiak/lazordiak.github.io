About Me

Hello! Hope you're doing well. I'm Schuyler. I graduated Vassar College in 2017 with a BA in Classics, another in Cognitive Science, and a minor in Art History. I was and am interested in exploring different ways of thinking, the different activities they inspire, generally just finding the most interesting ways of ramming ideas together at high speed. Mostly I do this by making things and then thinking about them, and some of those things end up here!

Currently, I'm studying as a Master's student at the ITP program at NYU. My interests include refactoring this website so it doesn't look awful and you, if you're interested in talking!

Electrons are always trying to get to the lowest energy state, this is something I learned in chemistry before I stopped taking chemistry. I think that people are like electrons, always trying to fall to the state where we can expend the least amount of energy trying to be who we are.

I would like to make things that help people find those spaces of low energy, in themselves and in the world.
