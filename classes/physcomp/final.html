Physical Computation Final - Liquid Relationships

This is the page for my physical computation final. It's dynamic, so I'll be updating it as my project evolves. I use water to express the relationships between people -- on approach, I have a series of pumps that make the water roil and churn. If people connect with each other physically in order to complete a circuit, the waters calm and an LED light show appears.
